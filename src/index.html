Coding is not a difficult task in the same way that learning the rules of chess is not a difficult task. Anyone can learn what a for-loop is. Anyone can learn what a while-loop is. Anyone can learn what a variable is. All just as easily as someone can learn how to move a pawn or a rook, but learning how to move a pawn or a rook -- or any of the other pieces -- does not mean you know how to play chess.
